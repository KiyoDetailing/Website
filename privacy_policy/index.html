1. What Information Do We Collect?

2. How Do We Process Your Information?

We process your information to:

  • Fulfill orders and manage transactions

3. What Legal Bases Do We Rely On?

Under GDPR and UK GDPR, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Consent: You’ve given clear permission.
  • Contract: Processing necessary to fulfil a contract.
  • Legitimate Interests: For business operations, provided your rights are not overridden.
  • Legal Obligations: Compliance with laws or regulations.
  • Vital Interests: Protection of life or safety.

6. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?
