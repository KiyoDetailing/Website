About Us

Based in the heart of South Shields, Kiyo Detailing is a mobile detailing company that is dedicated to restoring and maintaining the flawless beauty of your vehicle. Our name, inspired by the Japanese word "清" (Kiyo), meaning "clean" or "pure", reflects our commitment to precision, care, and unmatched attention to detail. Whether it's a full detail, exterior or interior refresh, we pride ourselves on delivering professional service with every clean. At Kiyo Detailing, your car isn't just a vehicle — it's a reflection of pride and craftsmanship.

Our Services

Please note: Our mobile services require access to the client's water and electricity supply.

  • Standard Exterior Detail

    £35

    1.5-2 Hours

    A full foam wash thoroughly cleans the paintwork, wheels, tires, and door jambs. Wheels and tires are deep cleaned to remove surface grime and brake dust, followed by a premium tire shine for a fresh, dressed look. This package excludes ceramic sealant and chemical decontamination.

  • Premium Exterior Detail

    £55

    1.5-2 Hours

    A thorough foam wash covers paintwork, wheels, tires, and door jambs, followed by chemical decontamination to remove tar and iron. We apply a high-quality ceramic sealant, providing up to 2 months of gloss and protection. Wheels and tires are deep cleaned and finished with a premium tire shine.

  • Interior Detail

    £30

    1.5 Hours

    The interior is thoroughly vacuumed, including cracks and crevices. All surfaces are wiped down, and leather or fabric seats are cleaned and refreshed. Glass is left streak-free, and all interior surfaces are treated for a clean, well-maintained finish.

  • Full Detail

    £55

    3 Hours

    Includes a full foam wash covering paintwork, wheels, tires, and door jambs. Wheels and tires are deep cleaned and finished with a premium tire shine. Interior surfaces are vacuumed (including crevices), wiped down, and seats cleaned. Glass is left streak-free for a refreshed, well-maintained finish.

  • Full Premium Detail

    £75

    3 Hours

    Includes a full foam wash with chemical decontamination to remove tar and iron, followed by a ceramic sealant for lasting gloss and protection. Wheels and tires are deep cleaned and dressed. The interior is vacuumed, seats are cleaned, and all surfaces—including glass—are left spotless and refreshed.

Add-ON
